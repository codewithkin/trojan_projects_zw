## Visual Identity

### Logo Usage
The Trojan Projects ZW logo should be displayed:
- With adequate white space around it
- At minimum 40px height for digital applications
- In full color for primary applications
- In monochrome for restricted applications

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, professional sans-serif (Recommended: Inter, Poppins)
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Accent**: Monospace for technical content

### Imagery Style
- Professional, modern photography
- Equipment and project showcases
- Team and customer testimonials
- Industrial and technical focus
